◆ su_stream_chunk()

enum su_error su_stream_chunk ( struct su_stream * stream,
struct su_chunk ** chunks,
struct su_chunk ** chunk_out )

Advances the stream by one chunk, allocating and appending a new su_chunk.

Allocates a new chunk, links it into the chunks list, and updates stream to point to the new chunk's embedded stream for subsequent operations.

Parameters
streamCurrent stream to advance from. Must not be NULL.
chunksHead of the chunk linked list. Must not be NULL.
chunk_outOut parameter receiving the newly allocated chunk. Must not be NULL.
Returns
SU_OK on success.
SU_MEMORY_ALLOCATION_FAILURE if the new chunk could not be allocated.
